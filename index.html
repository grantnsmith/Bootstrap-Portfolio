Hello,

I'm Grant.

I'm a full-stack web developer.

Nearing 30, my focus changed to future plans of a family, and a more tradtiional career path was appealing. Reaching out to my contacts in the tech world, I was able to transition back into the field as a Systems Admin at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure in Seattle, WA.

Wanting to "level up", I enrolled in a full-stack web development course through the University of Washington.
